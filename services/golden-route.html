The Golden Route: Generations & Discovery Tour

A 10-day luxury experience blending technology, tradition, and family bonding in Japan

Past, Present, & Play

A seamless journey through Japan's past, present, and play, designed for multi-generational families.

The Golden Route: Tokyo → Kyoto → Osaka - 10 Days of Discovery

Day 1-4: Tokyo

Tokyo - Tech & Pop Culture

4 Nights

Miraikan, teamLab Planets, Shibuya Sky, Harajuku. Stay: The Peninsula Tokyo

Immersive digital art at teamLab Planets and panoramic views from Shibuya Sky

Highlights:

  • Interactive science exhibits at Miraikan
  • Digital art immersion at teamLab Planets
  • 360° city views from Shibuya Sky
  • Harajuku fashion and culture exploration
Day 5-7: Kyoto

Kyoto - Tradition & Heritage

3 Nights

Kinkaku-ji, Arashiyama Bamboo Grove, Fushimi Inari, Private Tea Ceremony & Wagashi Workshop. Stay: The Ritz-Carlton, Kyoto

Golden Pavilion (Kinkaku-ji) and serene Arashiyama Bamboo Forest

Highlights:

  • Golden Pavilion (Kinkaku-ji) visit
  • Arashiyama Bamboo Forest walk
  • Fushimi Inari Shrine exploration
  • Private traditional tea ceremony
  • Wagashi (Japanese sweets) making workshop
Day 8-9: Osaka

Osaka - Fun & Farewell

2 Nights

Osaka Castle, Universal Studios Japan (with Express Pass), Dotonbori. Stay: InterContinental Osaka

Universal Studios Japan Nintendo World and historic Osaka Castle

Highlights:

  • Osaka Castle historical tour
  • Universal Studios Japan with Express Pass
  • Nintendo World exploration
  • Dotonbori street food adventure
  • Farewell family dinner
