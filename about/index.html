Our Mission

Playfluence = Play + Influence. Our name is our mission.

Our Mission

To connect people with meaningful recreational experiences by delivering creative, data-driven marketing solutions. We strive to promote zoos, safaris, and theme parks as spaces of learning, adventure, and sustainability.

Our Vision

To become China's leading creative marketing company that shapes the future of recreation through innovation, sustainability, and meaningful human connections.
