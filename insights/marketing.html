Sales Promotions Strategy

We offer strategic incentives that maintain our premium positioning while stimulating bookings and building customer loyalty.

Visual designs for our premium promotions maintaining brand positioning

Early Bird Offers

15-20% discount for bookings made 6 months in advance, with additional benefits like room upgrades or special experiences.

  • Secure advanced planning
  • Better resource allocation
  • Premium positioning maintained

Family Package Deals

Special rates for 3+ family members, with added value like complimentary activities or dining experiences.

  • Encourage group travel
  • Higher average booking value
  • Word-of-mouth amplification

Note: We focus on value-added promotions rather than simple discounts to maintain our premium brand positioning and perceived value.
