Our Commitments & Actions

Four pillars of our sustainable recreation strategy

1. Empowering Green Destinations

We prioritize partnerships with zoos holding certifications (e.g., AZA) and theme parks practicing green operations. We amplify their efforts through marketing, turning "sustainable options" into consumers' "preferred choices."

  • Green certification partnerships
  • Sustainable venue promotion
  • Eco-friendly operation support

2. Curating Responsible Experiences

In our product design (e.g., the Golden Route Tour), we incorporate low-carbon transport, support local community economies, select hotels and restaurants with sustainable sourcing policies.

  • Low-carbon transportation
  • Local community support
  • Sustainable hotel partnerships

3. Spreading Education & Awareness

We partner with top NGOs like WWF China to co-develop interactive educational content. We seamlessly integrate knowledge about biodiversity conservation into our campaigns.

  • WWF China partnership
  • Educational content creation
  • Biodiversity conservation focus

4. Measuring Our Footprint

We are committed to quantifying and reducing the environmental impact of our projects. We explore carbon offset options and regularly report on our progress in driving positive industry change.

  • Carbon footprint tracking
  • Regular impact reporting
  • Industry leadership

Sustainability in Action

How we integrate sustainability into our Golden Route Tour

Low-Carbon Transportation

We prioritize Japan's efficient Shinkansen (bullet trains) for inter-city travel, which produces significantly less carbon per passenger compared to domestic flights. All ground transportation uses hybrid or electric vehicles.

Green Hotel Partnerships

All hotels in our Golden Route Tour are selected based on their sustainability credentials, including energy efficiency, waste reduction programs, and local sourcing of food and materials.

Sustainable Dining

We collaborate with restaurants that prioritize seasonal, locally-sourced ingredients and minimize food waste. We also incorporate plant-based meal options to reduce environmental impact.

Community Engagement

Our tours include visits to local artisans and traditional workshops, supporting community economies while preserving cultural heritage through responsible tourism.
